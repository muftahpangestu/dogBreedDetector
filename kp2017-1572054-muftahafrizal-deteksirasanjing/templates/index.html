About this site

What is Convolutional Neural Network?

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is one of the best algorithm for Image Recognition. It was Inspired on how Human's Neural Network work when recognizing an object from what they see. CNN extract every feature that image have (in this case for a dog it could be ear, nose, mouth, etc.) during the training and use it to classify image during the testing. That is why CNN work quite well even for an image that has another object beside the dog on it.

We use pre-trained model that already provided by Keras, which is ResNet50, Xception and VGG16. Each model have different configuration and CNN architecture, so each of them can have different performa/accuracy.


Determining the Breed

As explained above, CNN models extract the feature from the image of dogs from various breeds. So they have general idea on how to classify and distinguish many kind of breeds that have trained beforehand. The feature extracted could be fur's length, color, figure and many others. Models determine how close the dog detected on the image to they general idea of the said breeds.
